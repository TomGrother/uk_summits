UK Summits

Your interactive checklist for the 189 Welsh Nuttalls

The Nuttalls are every mountain in Wales over 2,000ft (610m) with at least 15m of drop on all sides — 189 summits in total, from Snowdon down to the smallest subsidiary tops. UK Summits is a free tracker to help you explore them all.
